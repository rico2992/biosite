Personal Interests and Hobbies

Outside of her professional life, Nyissa is an avid crafter. She enjoys creating decorative baskets, trees, door wreaths, and other handmade items. Her love for crafting not only serves as a creative outlet but also reflects her patience and attention to detail, qualities that she brings to her counseling role.

Inspirations and Goals

Nyissa's passion for education and her desire to make a difference in the lives of students led her to become a career advisor. She integrates her crafting skills into her professional life, using creativity to engage and inspire her students. Raised in the Rio Grande Valley in a small town called Weslaco, Nyissa values her family deeply. She cherishes spending time with her family, a loving boyfriend, and their 18-month-old son when she is not working or crafting.
